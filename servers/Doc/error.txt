Error Messages OpenExodus FSM:

  001a = no carddock connected
  001b = not every carddock is connected (14)
  001c = there are more than 14 carddocks

  002a = not all cards are connected(modem/internet)
  002b = there are to many cards connected

  003a = no tunnel to backupserver is connected
  003b = there are more than one backupserver tunnel

  004a = backupserver is offline/dont responed
